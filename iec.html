International Education Careers

The Council of International Schools has more than 30 years experience helping school educators and leaders find opportunities in high quality schools around the world. Working with CIS provides educators with access to positions in more than 660 schools that offer an international education. Through the CIS Educator Database, an annual recruitment fair and custom leadership searches, educators and staff looking for new international positions can use CIS as a central resource.

School Teaching and Staff Positions

Educators who are looking for positions at CIS Member Schools can apply to become candidates with CIS and set up an online portfolio. Once approved, your portfolio can be accessed by CIS Member Schools looking to hire new educators and staff. In addition, educators may be invited to participate in the annual CIS Educator Recruitment Fair in London which brings leaders from more than 100 schools around the world to one convenient event.

School Leadership Positions

Member Schools work with CIS to promote leadership openings and to recruit specific senior leadership positions including Heads of Schools or Divisional Heads. School leaders are invited to take advantage of our free-of-charge recruitment service and establish an active portfolio which can be made available to schools searching for new leaders.

LEADERSHIP SEARCH

Why you should become a CIS Leadership Search Candidate & apply for your next leadership position

The membership community values CIS for its experience and guidance during times of transition. Our global team at CIS have first-hand experience in applying for leadership positions and conducting searches for schools and are thus trusted sources of support and guidance.

Working with CIS to apply for a leadership position means that you will have access to our extensive network of international schools (over 660 in 110 countries) that regularly use our services to seek their next school leader. Schools look to CIS to find qualified leadership candidates who share our organisational values.

School leaders looking for new and challenging international positions can work with CIS in several ways:

Affiliated Consultants

CIS Affiliated Consultant Members are individuals who are part of a global network of schools, higher education institutions, educators and organizations in more than 100 countries dedicated to quality international education. Through this unique membership opportunity, CIS refers Affiliated Consultants to CIS Member Schools seeking dedicated expertise with educational improvement projects.

CIS connects Affiliated Consultant Members with more than 660 Member Schools in more than 100 countries. Consultants have a unique opportunity to reach CIS members through member-only resources and outreach programs throughout the school year.

Membership Benefits

Global Reach of CIS Members

CIS connects Affiliate Members with more than 660 schools in over 100 countries and gives them the opportunity to expand their network and find new engagements. CIS Affiliate Members can reach other CIS members through member-only resources and outreach programs throughout the school year.

Working with Quality Schools and Organisations

When you work with CIS Member Schools, you know you are working with high quality schools dedicated to international education. Each CIS Member school and institution has completed an extensive membership application process.
