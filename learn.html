We help you to grow and adapt in uncertain times

Schools and systems around the world are trying to discover how to educate young people so that they acquire the knowledge, skills, attitudes, values, and capabilities to live as active, productive, responsible, and participative citizens in modern societies.The same applies to those owning, running, and working in these schools and systems.With ever-diminishing half-lives of knowledge and skills, schools cannot limit themselves to the transmission of set content. Instead, they need to develop and promote flexibility, openness to new ideas, ability to adapt, and courage in the face of the unexpected.

For Consultants & Supporting Organisations

CIS School and Higher Education Members look to CIS to help them identify and establish linkages with organisations and individuals who offer expertise in international education and who can support them in the development and enhancement of their programmes and services.

School Teaching and Staff Positions

Educators who are looking for positions at CIS Member Schools can apply to become candidates with CIS and set up an online portfolio. Once approved, your portfolio can be accessed by CIS Member Schools looking to hire new educators and staff. In addition, educators may be invited to participate in the annual CIS Educator Recruitment Fair in London which brings leaders from more than 100 schools around the world to one convenient event. Member Schools work with CIS to promote leadership openings and to recruit specific senior leadership positions including Heads of Schools or Divisional Heads. School leaders are invited to take advantage of our free-of-charge recruitment service and establish an active portfolio which can be made available to schools searching for new leaders.
