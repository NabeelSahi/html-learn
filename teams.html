Volunteer Committees & Teams

Members serving members is a vital aspect of CIS. The combined expertise of more than 3000 individuals representing over 1100 member schools and higher education institutions around the globe who serve on Volunteer Committees and Teams brings a wealth of experience and a truly international dimension to the work of the organisation. In turn, participation in CIS services and events provides colleagues in international education with valuable opportunities for professional and personal growth. Whether serving on an Accreditation Visiting Team, leading a University Counsellor Tour or a College and University Tour, or advising CIS on current research in international education, members supporting and connecting with other members is the core of the CIS mission.

Premium Membership

To qualify for Premium School Membership, your school must be accredited by a recognised agency and feature a:

  • curriculum in which the culture and educational system of two or more countries is represented;
  • curriculum typical of one country, but located in another country and actively pursuing cultural exchange with its host country; OR
  • diverse student body, educational aims, and curricular offerings which promote and support the values of CIS

Global Access Membership

Our newest level of membership is open to:

  • Startup/early stage international schools not yet accredited
  • State or independent schools offering an international programme or international curricular option within their curriculum structure

Member Benefits

Benefits are identical to premium school members, with the exceptions of reduced rates for learning events and the right to nominate trustees or vote on corporate business.Annual subscription is a flat fee of $1,500 (+VAT where applicable), and the membership period runs from 1 September to 31 August.
