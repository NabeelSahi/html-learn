Higher Education Committees

CIS committee members play an important role in the delivery of student recruitment events. The Committees have been established to leverage the expertise of individuals from member institutions who are experienced leaders in university admission and recruitment.The CIS Higher Education Committee serves as an advisory board for CIS and is comprised of representatives from CIS Regional Service Committees, the CIS Guidance Committee, as well as appointed and ex-officio members. The planning and leading of Student Recruitment Tours is conducted by the CIS Regional Service Committees serving each world region. The CIS Guidance Committee is a volunteer committee which supports the work of the CIS Regional Service Committees, plans and leads the annual CIS University Counsellor Tour and provides support for the CIS Forum.

Higher Education Committee

The CIS Higher Education Committee serves as an advisory board for the CIS Office of Higher Education.

Asia Committee

The CIS Asia Committee plans and conducts recruitment tours to Asia and South Asia.

Canadian Higher Education Committee (CHEC)

The CIS Canadian Higher Education Committee plans and conducts tours for Canadian higher education members.

Committee on Europe, the Middle East and Africa (CEMA)

The CIS Committee for Europe, the Middle East and Africa plans and conducts recruitment tours in Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Eurasia.

Latin America Committee

The CIS Latin America Committee plans and conducts recruitment tours in Central and South America.

United Kingdom (UK) Higher Education Committee

The CIS UK Higher Education Committee plans and conducts recruitment tours in Europe for UK higher education members

Guidance Committee

The CIS Guidance Committee supports CIS Regional Service and Higher Education Committees and plans and conducts the annual CIS University Counsellor Tour.
